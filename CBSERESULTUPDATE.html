How to Check CBSE Result

Follow these easy steps to check your board result online.

1

Open Official Website

Visit the official CBSE result website.

2

Select Your Class

Choose either Class 10 Result or Class 12 Result option.

3

Enter Details

Fill in your Roll Number, School Number, and Admit Card ID.

4

View & Download

Submit details and download your provisional mark sheet.

Pass / Compartment / Improvement status and overall percentage or grade.

Re-evaluation

If you are not satisfied with your marks, wait for official rechecking or verification notice.

Compartment Exam

Students who do not clear some subjects may get a compartment exam option as per CBSE rules.
